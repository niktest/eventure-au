Sunset Yoga 

Experience tranquility and rejuvenation as the sun sets over magnificent Omeo Park! Join us for Sunset Yoga by City of Cockburn 9 April 2026 to 14 May 2026 — a FREE, all-ages, all-abilities community yoga session like no other. Whether you’re a seasoned yogi or a first-time visitor, all are warmly welcome to roll out their mat and bask in the golden glow of sunset. 

Breathe, Stretch, and Find Your Inner Calm on the Grass 

Feel the fresh grass beneath your feet, inhale the crisp evening air, and move through gentle yoga postures guided by experienced instructors (no prior experience necessary!). This is your time to unwind, stretch, and reconnect with yourself — all with the stunning backdrop of Omeo Park’s natural beauty.

Key Highlights 

  • FREE and open to all! 
  • All ages & abilities 
  • Breathtaking sunset views over Omeo Park create a peaceful, memorable setting.
  • Bring your own yoga mat, water, and smile!

Perfect for families, friends, or solo explorers seeking a peaceful escape or a mindful way to close out the day. Reconnect with nature, meet new people, and cultivate a sense of community harmony under the open sky. 

Don’t Miss Out! Bring Your Mat & Find Your Zen

Mark your calendars and make sure to join us at Omeo Park for an unforgettable evening of yoga, mindfulness, and sunset magic. This is more than just a yoga class — it’s an experience, a gathering, and a celebration of wellbeing for everyone in Cockburn and beyond!
