🎸🔥 KROME ROCK N RODS 2-DAY FESTIVAL 🔥🎸

Do you have products that perfectly fit the Rockabilly Revival?

Get ready to roll into one of the most anticipated Rockabilly festivals of 2026! 🚙💨
The chrome will shine, engines will roar, and the music will rock as we transform Laidley Showgrounds into a retro paradise bursting with rhythm, nostalgia, and style.

If your products scream vintage, rock ‘n’ roll, pin-up, retro, or classic cool, this is your stage to shine. 🌟

💃🕺 Everyone loves the music, dancing, and high energy — but this year, it’s all about taking it up a notch.
So dust off those swing frocks, polish those dancing shoes, and get ready to showcase your rockin’ products to thousands of excited festival-goers! 🎶✨

🎤🎶 WHAT’S ON & ATTRACTIONS

You won’t want to miss this jam-packed weekend featuring:
🎸 The Curation Specialists Rockabilly Australia Music Line-up
👢 Line Dance Workshops – learn to kick, twist, and groove!
💋 Community Pin-Up Competition & Fashion Parade
🚗 Hot Rods, Classics & Car Displays
🎪 Plus nostalgic vibes, vintage stalls, and rockin’ good times all weekend long!

This festival will be buzzing with colour, chrome, and classic cool — a must-attend event for lovers of Rockabilly, cars, fashion, and fun!

💥 WHAT WE’RE LOOKING FOR

We’re curating a top-tier lineup of quality vendors who capture the heart and soul of the Rockabilly & Nostalgia scene:


👗 Vintage & Rockabilly fashion
👠 Retro footwear & accessories
🎶 Vinyl, records & music memorabilia
🚙 Classic car gear, hot rod merch & collectables
🕶️ Homewares, art, pin-up, and all things vintage cool

🎯 Only the best of the best will make it — and we know our crowd loves stalls that sparkle with personality, passion, and quality!

Please complete the EOI below for your interest and attendance. Once approved an email will be generated with your access code to "SECURE & PAY" for you stall. Full details will be attached to the email.

🍔 Food Truck EOI

Details to be released soon — but get ready for mouth-watering opportunities to keep our hungry dancers, cruisers, and Show ’N’ Shine crew fueled up all weekend long! 😋🔥

📍 Venue: Laidley Showgrounds – 2 MacGregor Street, Laidley QLD
🗓️ Dates:
Saturday 29th August 2026 – 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
Sunday 30th August 2026 – 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM

💰 Stall Fees:

  • 3x3 m – $100 per day

  • 6x3 m – $180 per day

  • 9x3 m – $250 per day
    (Other sizes POA)

⛺ Camping & van sites available on-site – details to be released soon.

📧 Contact: info@uniqueepicevents.com.au

🔥 This isn’t just another market — it’s a full-blown festival experience!
With live bands, dance floors, classic cars, pin-up competitions, and Rockabilly lovers from all over, vendors will be lining up for this one.

Spaces are limited and interest is already revving up — so don’t wait! Secure your spot early and join the most rockin’ festival of the year! 🎤🎶💋

About Us:
At Unique Epic Events, we specialize in crafting unforgettable experiences that are as bold, creative, and extraordinary as our clients. From vibrant festivals and themed expos to intimate gatherings and large-scale markets, our events bring people together to celebrate diversity, passion, and creativity.

With a flair for the unique and a dedication to excellence, we curate events that captivate the imagination and leave a lasting impression. Whether it’s a gothic-themed expo, a retro rockabilly showcase, or a family-friendly market, we strive to create spaces where every attendee feels welcomed, inspired, and engaged.

Our Mission:
To deliver standout events that celebrate creativity, individuality, and community, offering unforgettable experiences for attendees and valuable opportunities for vendors.

Our Vision:
To be recognized as the leading event organizer for unique, inclusive, and inspiring experiences that bring communities together and highlight the extraordinary.

What We Do:

  • Themed Events: Gothic, retro, mystical, and everything in between – we bring bold and imaginative themes to life.
  • Expos: Showcasing a curated selection of vendors, creators, and performers in vibrant, engaging environments.
  • Tailored Experiences: Crafting custom events designed to meet specific needs, whether for private functions or public celebrations.

Why Choose Us?

  • Creative Vision: We think outside the box to ensure every event is distinctive and memorable.
  • Professional Expertise: Our team of seasoned event organizers ensures every detail is managed flawlessly.
  • Inclusive Atmosphere: We create spaces that welcome people from all walks of life, celebrating diversity and individuality.
  • Community-Focused: We prioritize supporting small businesses, local artisans, and community engagement.
